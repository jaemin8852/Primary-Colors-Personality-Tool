Primary Colors Personality Tool

There are seven rows of boxes. First, read Row 1 and choose the set of words that sounds the most like you. Click the checkbox.
Then read through the rest of the boxes in that row and choose the next set of words that sounds the most like you. Click the checkbox.
Keep going within each row until each box has a number, down to the number 1.
The box with the number 6 is the most like you. The box with the number 1 is the least like you.
After you complete Row 1, move on to Row 2 and do the same thing.
Then Row 3, Row 4 and so on, until you have done this for all seven rows.
